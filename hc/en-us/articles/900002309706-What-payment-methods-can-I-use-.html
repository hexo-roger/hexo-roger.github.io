What payment methods can I use?

The following payment methods are accepted.

  • Credit card payments (Visa/Mastercard)
  • PayPal

* If you wish to pay using a JCB card or a virtual debit card issued by Minna Bank, pixiv Branch, please proceed via PayPal.
* Please note that PayPal payments cannot be used for R-18 and R-18G requests at this time.

Furthermore, depending on your country or region, there are cases when all payment methods are unavailable and/or requests of R-18/R-18G works cannot be made. We thank you for your understanding.
