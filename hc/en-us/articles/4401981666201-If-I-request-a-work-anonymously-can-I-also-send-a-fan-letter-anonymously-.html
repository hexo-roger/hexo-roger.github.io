If I request a work anonymously, can I also send a fan letter anonymously?

When you send a request anonymously, your fan letter will also be anonymous. Your information won't be displayed to the creator or any other users. Co-requesting a work anonymously follows this pattern as well.

When an anonymous fan letter is sent, it will display as follows on the request details page:

  • To the person who sent the fan letter: "You anonymously sent a fan letter"
  • To all other users: "An anonymous user sent a fan letter"
