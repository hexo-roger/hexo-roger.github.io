What is Notification ?

Notification is a function used to notify users of new comments, tags or bookmarks on posted contents as well as new followers and My pixiv approvals in an easy, comprehensible manner.

Notifications are sent through the Notification at regular intervals when the contents are "bookmarked" or "liked".
If the type and contents for the notification are same, the old notifications are overwritten with new notifications so the pop-up will not be filled with duplicate notifications.

  • Notification Interval for Likes: For each item from 1 to 10, for every 10 items from 11 to 100, for every hundred items from 101 to 1,000, for every thousand items from 1,001 to 10,000 and for every ten thousand times when they exceed 10,000.
  • Bookmark Notification Interval: For each item from 1 to 10, for every 10 items from 11 to 100, for every hundred items from 101 to 1,000, for every thousand items from 1,001 to 10,000 and for every ten thousand times when they exceed 10,000.
