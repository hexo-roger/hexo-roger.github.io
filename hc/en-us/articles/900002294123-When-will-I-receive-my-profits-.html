When will I receive my profits?

Profits from requests posted between the 1st to the end of each month will be processed within 5 business days (excluding weekends and bank holidays) from the 20th of the following month.
* Depending on your bank, it may take a few days for the funds to show up in your account

The date of your initial request post will determine the payment month, even if it is still processing after the month has ended.
However, please do not submit incomplete works in order to expedite the month in which your payment will be made. Be aware that such behavior is considered misconduct and we may take action, such as suspending your account.

For profits of less than 5,000 JPY, you can either request a transfer from your Money management page, or the profits will be automatically transferred into your account in the month prior to 180 days from the date you received the request.
