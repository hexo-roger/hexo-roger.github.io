When will I get my refund?

● If your request has not yet been accepted, your credit transaction will be canceled.
It may take up to a week before these changes are reflected on your statement.

* There has been no transaction at this point when using credit as your payment method. We do not hold the money in escrow and there won't be a record in your card's transaction history.
* The process when using debit or prepaid cards as your payment method varies depending on the financial institution or issuing company. It's possible that you will have been credited the amount of the request.
It may take up to a week for the refund to show up after the request has been canceled, depending on the refund process with your issuing card company.

● If your request has already been accepted, a refund will be processed.
It may take up to a week before these changes are reflected on your statement.

* The timing and form of your refund may differ depending on your payment method and the progress made on the request.
If your refund hasn't been processed within a few days of cancellation, please contact your credit card company or PayPal for assistance.
