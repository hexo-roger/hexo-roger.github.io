I want to edit a work I posted to pixiv

▶On desktop
▶On mobile


On desktop

  1. Please open your Dashboard.
  2. Go to the "Works" tab and click the pencil icon next to the work you want to edit.
  3. After editing that work's information, click the "Save" button in the bottom portion of the screen.
  4. You will get a confirmation menu. Please click "OK" to update your work's information.


On mobile

  1. Please open your Dashboard.
  2. Go to the "Works" tab and tap the pencil icon next to the work you want to edit.
  3. After editing that work's information, tap the "Save" button in the bottom portion of the screen (for novels, the button is titled "Update").
