I don't receive the verification e-mail when registering to pixiv

The verification e-mail will be sent from email address [no-reply@pixiv.net].
Be sure to add the above e-mail address to your e-mail account’s list of safe senders.

Also double check to make sure that the e-mail address is entered correctly.

In addition, your junk e-mail/spam filter settings may cause the e-mail to be blocked, or send the e-mail to your junk e-mail/spam folder.

If you do not receive the verification e-mail after a period of time, check the following:

▪ If the e-mail is not delivered to your phone’s e-mail address

  • Are domain specific access control and address specific access control set?
  • Do you use your cellphone service provider’s junk mail blocker service?
  • Is the transmission of e-mail delayed because of reception problems?

* To verify the relevant settings operations, refer the manual that came with your phone, or contact the cell phone company.

If the e-mail is not delivered to your PC’s address

  • Is there a transmission delay on the sender-side e-mail service provider?
  • Was the e-mail sorted into the junk e-mail/spam folder?
  • Has the sender’s e-mail server exceeded its capacity?
  • Is the sender’s e-mail server experiencing technical difficulties?
  • Has the sender’s e-mail service provider detected the message to be junk e-mail/spam and deleted the message?
  • Is the transmission of e-mail delayed because of reception problems?

* For information and the operation for changing settings, contact the PC manufacturer or provider.

You can resend a verification email from the following page.
https://www.pixiv.net/setting_user.php
