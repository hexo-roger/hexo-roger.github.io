How are the copyrights handled for work linked with Tumeng?

The copyright of any work published on Tumeng belongs to the creator.

Additionally, to protect the rights of all their creators, Tumeng has a dedicated team and in-house lawyers.

If by some chance, you discover that your work has been reposted without authorization after posting on Tumeng, please use pixiv’s Contact Us page. In cooperation with our team, Tumeng has a dedicated response team working through a specialized law firm.
