How can I check the status of works on Tumeng?

Check the Manage Tumeng linkage page for the status of works on Tumeng.
* The Manage Tumeng linkage page can be displayed after linking your account with Tumeng, clicking “Linked services” in your account settings, and selecting “Check details” under “Tumeng linkage”.

The types of post statuses are as follows:

  • Posted:
    Currently, the work is posted to Tumeng.
  • OFF:
    The work has not been posted to Tumeng. (Ex: a deleted post or posting not selected)
  • Under review:
    Waiting for the results of Tumeng review.
  • Not posted as it didn't meet the Guidelines.:
    The work couldn’t be posted.
    * You can check the review criteria here
  • Can't post because of privacy settings:
    The work can’t be posted due to either being Private, limited to My pixiv, or URL restricted.
  • Can't post R-18 and R-18G works:
    The work can not be posted either because it’s been set as R-18 or R-18G.
