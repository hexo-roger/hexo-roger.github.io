The page displays an error and I can't leave pixiv

  1. Depending on the status of your FANBOX or BOOTH account, or any other pixiv related service, you may not be able to leave pixiv. View the details here.

  2. You cannot leave pixiv if you have a pixiv Points balance leftover from the Shoutout feature.
    If you still have a pixiv Points balance, please contact us from the inquiry form here.

  3. If you use app payments and some mobile carrier payments on pixiv Premium, you will not be able to delete your pixiv account.
    For more details in regards to unsubscribing from pixiv Premium, click here.

  4. If you have any in-progress requests or uncompleted payouts, you cannot leave pixiv. Please try again when all actions related to Requests are completed.
