I want to put my novel's title on the cover

You can set the title to display on the cover through the cover template settings reachable from the novel posting page.
You cannot upload images from the app.

 

If you want to upload your own cover image

Please take a look at the process below.
Is the novel cover an illustration you made yourself?


▶︎On desktop
▶︎On mobile
▶︎App version

On desktop

Please select the cover template you want from the list.

 

If you click "See all", then all of the cover templates will display.

 

The title displayed on the image can be edited by clicking "Cover settings".
The button is displayed to the right of "Cover preview".

 

You can enable and disable the display of the title, the title text displayed, the font, and the color via the "Cover settings" menu.

 

 

 

On mobile

Please select the cover template you want from the list.

 

If you click "See all", then all of the cover templates will display.

 

The title displayed on the image can be edited by clicking "Cover settings".
The button is displayed to the right of "Cover preview".

 

You can enable and disable the display of the title, the title text displayed, the font, and the color via the "Cover settings" menu.

 

▶︎App version

You can not edit the cover title from the app. Additionally, you can not insert a title on desktop or mobile for covers set from the app.
