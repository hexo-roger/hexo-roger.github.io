I would like to know how to search for content on pixiv.

▪ To search for AAA
Enter: AAA
Enter a keyword and click the Search button.

[AND searches]
▪ To search for content that contains two keywords, "AAA" and "BBB"
Enter: AAA BBB
Enter a space between the keywords and then click the Search button.

[Minus search]
▪ To exclude content that contains the keyword "CCC" from the search results of content that contains the keyword "AAA"
Enter: AAA -CCC
Enter a "-" in front of the keyword that you would like to exclude from the search results and click the Search button.
* Make sure that you enter a single-byte dash

[OR searches]
▪ To search for content that contains the keyword "AAA" or "BBB"
Enter: AAA OR BBB
Enter the word OR between keywords and then click the Search button.
* Make sure that you enter OR by using single-byte characters.

[Keywords + OR searches]
▪ To search for content that includes "AAA" as well as either "BBB" or "CCC"
Enter: AAA (BBB OR CCC)
Enter text structured like the search above and click the Search button. (* You must enter a space before and after the parentheses.)

Uppercase, lowercase, double-byte, and single-byte characters are not differentiated.
For example, a search for both "AAA" and "aaa" will yield the same search results.

[Size-specific searches]
▪ You can specify the size of the content you would like to search for. The following are each sizes that can be searched for.
Small (1,000 px x 1,000 px or smaller)
Medium (1,001 px x 1,001 to 3,000 px x 3,000 px)
Large (3,000 px x 3,000 px or larger)
Portrait (Allows you to search for content that is longer vertically than horizontally.)
Landscape (Allows you to search for content that is longer horizontally than vertically.)

[Time period specified content search]
▪ Allows you to search for content posted during a specified period of time.

[Proportions search]
▪ Allows you to specify searches for content that has portrait, landscape, or square proportions.

[Tool-specified search]
▪ Allows you to search for content created using a specific tool such
as SAI, Photoshop, Metasequoia, a mechanical pencil, or a regular pencil to name a few.

[User search]
▪ On the search results page, select the [User] tab.
