I cannot see my purchase information on the iOS App

If you have registered and paid for a Premium account through your iOS App but are unable to use the Premium features, please uninstall the App once before re-installing it and restoring your purchase information.
Learn how to restore your purchase information from the Premium Registration page in Settings on My Page.
