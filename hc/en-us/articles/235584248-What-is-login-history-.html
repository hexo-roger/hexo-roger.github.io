What is login history?

Login history is a function that displays the pixiv login dates and times or IP address for your account. You can check to make sure your account hasn't been used by anyone other than you. Please refer to following contents displayed.

If there is a record of an unknown login, you can log out all the terminals by changing your password. You can change your password here.

Time The time pixiv was accessed.
Service A record of the services you are logged into
When logging in on the PC version: pixiv (desktop version)
When logging in on smartphone version: pixiv (smartphone version)
When logging in with the iOS or Android app: Smartphone app
When logging into BOOTH, pixivFactory or pixiv comic: Related services
Connection source IP address ※ The IP address accessing pixiv is recorded

* Connection source IP address is the identification number that is assigned by your Internet service provider when you access the Internet. Please contact your Internet service provider to look up your IP address.
* It does not record Facebook login or Google login history. Please refer to login histories provided by Facebook or Google.
