What is a "workspace"?

The word "workspace" indicates your environment when creating works (the PC you're using, the model of your pen tablet, etc.).
Registering a workspace is optional.

Desktop version

After logging in, you can proceed to "Edit workspace" by clicking the link at the bottom of the "Edit profile" window.

Mobile version

After logging in, you can access the "Workspace" tab from the "Edit profile" button on the profile page.
