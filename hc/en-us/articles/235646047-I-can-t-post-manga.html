I can't post manga

If a manga fails to upload, check the following:

・ Are any of the files damaged?
・ Is each page under 32 MB and is the total manga work under 200 MB?

If that doesn't resolve your issue, please try the following:

・ Making sure you have a good internet connection before trying to upload again
・ Resaving all of the files (if any are damaged, you won't be able to post them)
・ Testing to see if it's possible to post a small number of the pages (if it works, gradually increase the number of pages to see if you can determine where the problem is)
・ Clearing your cache and cookies
・ Updating your browser to the latest version
・ Restarting your device

You can also try switching to another browser.
We recommend the following browsers.
---------------------------------
Latest version of Google Chrome
http://www.google.com/chrome/

Latest version of Firefox
https://www.mozilla.org/ja/firefox/new/
---------------------------------
When posting from an iPad
You cannot upload data directly from cloud services like iCloud.
Please try saving the files locally, to a folder like your photo library, and then uploading again.
It's also possible that you may not be able to upload due to your data plan or your device's specs. In this case, we ask that you upload from a PC.
