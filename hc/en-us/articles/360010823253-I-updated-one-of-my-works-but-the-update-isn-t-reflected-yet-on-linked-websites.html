I updated one of my works, but the update isn't reflected yet on linked websites

When you make a change to the title, replace an image, add a cover, etc to works that have also been shared to external sites (like Twitter), the displayed information may remain as it was before you made the change.

It may take a while for updated information to be reflected on external websites. There's nothing pixiv can do to fix this, so consider checking the information again after some time has passed.
