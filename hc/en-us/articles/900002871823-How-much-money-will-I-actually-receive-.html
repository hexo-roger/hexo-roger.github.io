How much money will I actually receive?

After your profits are set, the service usage fee and the transfer fee will be deducted.

Service usage fee
10% of profits

Transfer fee
In the case of a bank transfer, the transfer fee will vary depending on the amount of money to be transferred.
30,000 JPY or more → 300 JPY
Less than 30,000 JPY → 200 JPY

There is no transfer fee for PayPal payments.


[Example]
If your profits for this month are 5,000 JPY...

Service usage fee: 500 JPY (5,000*0.1)
　* The amount is rounded to the nearest yen.
Transfer fee: 200 JPY for bank transfer, free for PayPal

Actual amount you will receive: 4,300 JPY for bank transfer, 4,500 JPY for PayPal
