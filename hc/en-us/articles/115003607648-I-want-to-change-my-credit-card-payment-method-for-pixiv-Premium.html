I want to change my credit card payment method for pixiv Premium

  • If you'd like to delete a credit card registered as a payment method for pixiv Premium
    You can check the credit card info and delete it here.
    * If your pixiv Premium membership is processing for renewal, or you have an overdue payment, you will not be able to delete your credit card information.


  • If you'd like to change or update a credit card registered as a payment method for pixiv Premium
    Depending on the plan you subscribed to, the procedures for revising your credit card information are as follows:

    If you've subscribed to 1-month plan:
    Go to your Premium Membership Subscription page > "Update" under the payment method category > Enter new card information > “Change” > and your change is complete

    If you've subscribed to the 3-month, 6-month, or 12-month plans:
    Go to your Premium Membership Subscription page > "Update" under the payment method category > Add a new card information or select the card you want to use > “Use the selected card” > and your change is complete

    * If you change the credit card currently associated as your Premium Membership payment method, you will be billed using the new information when it's time to renew your membership.
