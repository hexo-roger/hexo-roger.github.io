How can I rename Bookmark tags?

You can rename tags from the desktop version of pixiv only, and not from mobile or app.

 

  1. From the home page, click on your icon in the top right corner, and then Bookmarks.


  2. Call up the bookmark tags list by clicking on the ・・・ icon on the right-hand side, or below the bookmark tags.


  3. Click on "Edit tags", and you will be prompted to the editing mode.


  4. Click on the pencil icon for any tag to rename it.
