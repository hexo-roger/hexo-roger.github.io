How do I manage requests?

Open the Manage requests page to check received and sent requests, as well as your Request terms.
https://www.pixiv.net/manage/requests

Requests you received or sent are divided into two tabs: "All" and "Canceled".
Each request will be shown with one of the following statuses:

Received requests
All:

  • Pending approval
  • In progress
  • In review
  • Completed
  • Resend offer pending

Canceled:

  • Canceled
  • Canceled/Resendable request

Sent requests
All:

  • Pending approval
  • In progress
  • In review
  • Completed
  • Request resendable

Canceled:

  • Canceled

 

In addition, you can also filter or sort requests by their status.
