I want to check my viewing history.

■ About browsing history

  • For pixiv Premium users
    You can check your browsing history for the last 180 days and up to 10,000 results.
  • For general users
    In order to use the browsing history feature, you will need to register for pixiv Premium.
    After registering for pixiv Premium, you can check your browsing history for the past 180 days, including the time before your registration date, and up to 10,000 results.

    You can register for pixiv Premium here.
    (After registering for pixiv Premium, your browsing history prior to your Premium registration may take a few hours to load.)

 

< Desktop version >

You can check your browsing history from the area beneath your profile picture on the right side of the Home page.

Works that you have liked will be displayed in your browsing history with a smile icon in the bottom right corner. Works that you have bookmarked will be displayed in your browsing history with a heart icon in the bottom right corner.

 

< Mobile version >

You can check your browsing history from the menu at the top left of the Home page.

Works that you have bookmarked will be displayed in your browsing history with a heart icon in the bottom right corner.

 

< App >

You can check your browsing history from My Page.

Works that you have bookmarked/liked will be displayed in your browsing history with a heart icon in the bottom right corner.
