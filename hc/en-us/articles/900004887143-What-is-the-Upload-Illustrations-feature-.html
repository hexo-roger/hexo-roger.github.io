What is the Upload Illustrations feature?

The Upload Illustrations feature is used to directly upload illustrations and images to posts of novel works.


Take a look below if you'd like to learn how to use this feature.

▶︎On desktop

▶︎On mobile

On desktop

Click the "Tool" drop-down menu on the novel posting page, then click "Upload illustrations".

 

Please upload your images via the menu that pops up. You can upload multiple images at a time.

 

You can change the order of the images as well as delete them by clicking the "Edit" button.

 

When you've finished uploading the images, the uploaded image tags will be added to your work.

 

The location of the tag inside your work is where the illustrations will be displayed.

 

On mobile

Click the "Tool" drop-down menu on the novel posting page, then click "Illustration (upload)".

 

Please upload your images via the menu that pops up. You can upload multiple images at a time.



You can change the order of the images as well as delete them by clicking the "Edit" button.

 

When you've finished uploading the images, the uploaded image tags will be added to your work.

 

The location of the tag inside your work is where the illustrations will be displayed.
