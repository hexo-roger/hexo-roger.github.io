The tags of the work is not displayed in the preview on the "Manage Tumeng linkage page"

The tags of the work will be reviewed separately from the work itself, and those tags that have passed the review process will be displayed on Tumeng. If there are tags that are awaiting review or have not passed the review process, those tags will not be posted, but the work itself will be posted.
Currently, tags are being reviewed by Tumeng, and those that have passed the review process will be posted on Tumeng in order. In addition, there may be a delay of up to about one day from the time the work is posted until the tag is displayed. You can check the status of the tags from the preview on the "Manage Tumeng linkage page".
