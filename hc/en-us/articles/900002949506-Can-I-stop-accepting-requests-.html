Can I stop accepting requests?

Yes, you can stop at any time from the page you use to edit your Terms.
When you stop requests, fans will no longer be able to send you new ones.

If you delete or close a set of Terms, it will not affect any requests you may have ongoing. The requests will still be valid with the content and price set at the time you accepted them, and will not be automatically canceled.
